Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • ½ teaspoon cream of tartar
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 ¼ cups flour
  • colored sugars
  • 1 egg

Directions

Mix butter, sugar, oil, and egg. Add dry ingredients until blended. Roll into balls and press down with a glass to flatten. Sprinkle with colored sugar. Bake at 350° for 10 minutes or until slightly golden.
