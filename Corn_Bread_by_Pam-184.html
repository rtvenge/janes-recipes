Ingredients

  • 2 cups Bisquick mix
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons corn meal
  • 1 ½ sticks butter-melted/cooled
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Directions

Mix all ingredients until blended. Bake at 350° in an 8 x 8 greased glass pan for approximately 25 minutes.
