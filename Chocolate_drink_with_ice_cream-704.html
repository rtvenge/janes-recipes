Ingredients

  • 1 cup chocolate ice cream
  • ¼ cup Baileys
  • 2 tablespoons dark cream de cacao
  • 2 tablespoons vodka

Directions

Put in blender. Pour into martini glass what has been rimmed with chocolate and chocolate sprinkles.
