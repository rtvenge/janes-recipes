Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion-chopped
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • ⅓ cup cider vinegar
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons mustard
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 ¼ teaspoons pepper

Directions

Combine in food processor until blended. Simmer on the stove for approximately 30 minutes for flavors to blend. If you do not want to use the processor, grate the onion and the blend in the remaining ingredients.
