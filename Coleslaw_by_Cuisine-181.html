Ingredients

  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 3 tablespoons cider vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • ½ teaspoon Kosher salt
  • 3 cups shredded cabbage
  • 2 cups shredded Granny Smith Apples

Directions

Whisk together first five ingredients. Combine with the remaining ingredients.
