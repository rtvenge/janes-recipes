Butterscotch Ice Cream Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ⅔ cup white corn syrup
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ⅔ cup evaporated milk
  • ⅛ teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions

Mix together baking soda and vanilla. Set aside. In saucepan, mix together brown sugar, corn syrup, butter and milk. Over medium heat, stirring constantly, cook until the mixture comes to a rolling boil. Let boil, unstirred for one minute. Remove from heat, add soda mixture. Refrigerate.
