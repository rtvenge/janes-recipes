Ingredients

  • ½ cup sesame oil
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon hot chili oil
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1-2 teaspoon sesame seeds

Directions

Mix together except the seed. Add your meat, veggies, and stir fry. Add cooked noodles. Pour over sauce. Top with seeds, green onions if you want.
