Buffalo Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 12 or 2 pounds chicken wings
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • ¼ cup flour
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 3 teaspoons red pepper sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • celery/blue cheese dip

Directions

Heat oven to 425°. Melt 2 tablespoons in 13x9x2 pan. Coat chicken in flour, shake off extra flour. Place in pan. Bake uncovered 20 minutes, turn chicken. Bake uncovered 10-12 minutes longer.
Mix one tablespoon butter, vinegar, pepper sauce and salt in large bowl until blended. Add chicken and toss. Serve with celery and blue cheese.
