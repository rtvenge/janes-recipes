Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ⅔ cup oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard , dry
  • 1 teaspoon celery seed
  • 1 cup cider vinegar
  • fresh pepper to taste
  • onion-grated

Directions

Boil, and simmer until the sugar dissolved
