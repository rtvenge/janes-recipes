Ingredients

  • Lemon cake mix-Betty Crocker
  • 1 cup water
  • ½ cup oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons poppy seeds
  • Lemon juice
  • powdered sugar

Directions

Grease and flour pan. Preheat oven to 325°. Make the cake as per instructions on the box. Add poppy seed. Bake 60-70 minutes. Cool 20 minutes before removing from pan.
Make a frosting combining sugar and juice.
