Chocolate chocolate chip muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • ¾ cups sugar
  • ¾ cup chocolate chips, divided
  • 1 cup milk
  • ⅓ cup plus 2 teas vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions

Preheat oven to 400 °. Mix dry ingredients (not chips) together. Mix all wet ingredients together in bowl until blended. Add dry mixture, will be lumpy. Add 1/2 cup chocolate chips to batter. Put in greased muffin tins and sprinkle evenly with remaining 1/4 cup chips. Bake for approximately 20 minutes.
