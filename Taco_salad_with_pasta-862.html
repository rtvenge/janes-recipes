Taco salad with pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 cups spiral pasta noodles, uncooked
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pkg taco seasoning
  • 3 cups shredded lettuce
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup onion, chopped
  • ½ cup French salad dressing
  • 7 ounces bag of corn chips
  • 2 tablespoons sour cream

Directions

Cook pasta and drain. Cook beef and drain. Add seasoning and cool. Mix beef mixture into pasta;, toss lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, onion, dressing, and chips into mixture. Top with sour cream.
