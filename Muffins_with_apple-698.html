Muffins with apple

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 ¼ cups sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups shredded carrots
  • ½ cup raisins
  • ½ cup unsweetened flake coconut
  • 1 apple , peeled, cored, shredded
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla

Directions

Mix together flour, sugar, soda, cinnamon, and salt. Stir in carrot, raisins, coconut, and apple. In separate bowl beat together eggs, oil, and vanilla. Stir egg mixture into dry mixture. Scoop into greased or paper lined muffin tins. Bake at 350° for approximately 20 minutes.
