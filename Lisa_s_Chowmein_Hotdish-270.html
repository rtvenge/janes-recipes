Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • 1 large onion-chopped
  • 1 cup celery
  • 2 cans cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 cans chicken rice soup
  • 1 can mixed vegetables
  • 1 can whole muchrooms
  • 2 cups chowmein noodles
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

Directions

Brown beef, onion and celery, drain if needed. Drain veggies and mushrooms. Mix all together and bake one hour at 350°.
