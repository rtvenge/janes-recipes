Ingredients

  • 2 boxes tapioca pudding
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1 can fruit cocktail
  • 1 can pineapple , crushed
  • 1 can mardarin oranges
  • 1 package mini marshmellows
  • 1 cup whipping cream
  • 2 bananas

Directions

Boil pudding mix and milk, cool. Fold in other ingredients. Add bananas slices just before serving.
