Ingredients

  • 2 pounds gr beef
  • 15 ounces chili block
  • 1 onion-chopped
  • 2 16 oz stewed tomatoes
  • 1 15 tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato soup
  • 1-2 teaspoon oregano
  • 2 cloves garlic minced

Directions

Dissolve the chili block in 1/2 cup water, brown beef, onion, garlic and drain. Add all other ingredients, simmer one hour. Serve over noodles.
