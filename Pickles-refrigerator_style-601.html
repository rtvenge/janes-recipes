Ingredients

  • 7 cups cucumbers, sliced
  • 1 cup onion, sliced
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 tablespoons pickling salt
  • 1 teaspoon celery seeds

Directions

Mix all together, put in the refrigerator overnight. Ready to eat. Will keep up to one years. Use a glass container or containers.
