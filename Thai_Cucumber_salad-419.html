Thai Cucumber salad

Ingredients

  • ½ red onion-chopped
  • 1 red jalapeno-chopped
  • 1 English cucumber
  • 6 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon Kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons cilantro-chopped
  • optional 1 1/2 teas fish sauce

Directions

Slice cucumber thin and 1/2 the slices. Sprinkle with salt and a very small amount of sugar and let set for a couple hours. Mix together with Jalapeno and onion. Whisk together the remaining ingredients and add to cucumber mixture. Chill.
