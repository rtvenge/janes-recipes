Ingredients

  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 teaspoons dill weed
  • 2 teaspoons Beau Monde
  • 1 tablespoon grated onion

Directions

In processor, blend yogurt and cottage cheese until smooth. Stir in onion and spices. Adjust with salt if needed.
