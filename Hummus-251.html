Ingredients

  • 4 cloves garlic-minced
  • 19 ounces garbanzo beans
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon pepper
  • dash hot sauce
  • ¼ cup olive oil

Directions

In processor, combine first six ingredients until smooth. While the processor is going, add the olive through the shoot.
