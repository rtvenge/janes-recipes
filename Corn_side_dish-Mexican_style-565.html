Corn side dish-Mexican style

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen corn, kernels
  • ¾ cup chopped fresh tomatillos
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon pickled jalapenos, or more
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • ¼ teaspoon sugar
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

Saute corn, tomatillos, onion, and jalapeno in oil over medium high heat until tender. Approximately 5 minutes.

Stir in cream, cilantro, and sugar; cook until reduced and thickened, 5-6 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
