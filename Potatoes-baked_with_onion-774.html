Potatoes-baked with onion

Ingredients

  • baking potatoes
  • onion
  • butter
  • garlic
  • olive oil spray
  • salt

Directions

Wash potatoes and cut potatoes 1/2 inch thick wedged-not all the way through. Stuff with onion slices, butter, garlic. Spray with olive oil. Top with salt and pepper. Roll up in foil and bake 90 minutes.
