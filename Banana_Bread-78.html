Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup butter or butter flavored crisco
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 ½ cups mashed bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup buttermilk , (or put in 1 T lemon juice in milk

Directions

Preheat oven to 350. Cream butter. Add sugar gradually. Blend in eggs, banana and vanilla. Blend together flour, salt and soda. Add alternately with buttermilk. Blend just until well combined. Pour into large loaf pan-approximately 1 hour for baking. Less time if small pans are used.
