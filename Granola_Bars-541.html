Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons ground flaxseed
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cups old-fashioned oats
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ cup raisins

Directions

In a large bowl, beat butter and brown sugar until crumbly, about 2 minutes. Add egg; beat well. Stir in flax and honey. In a small bowl, combine the oats, flour, cinnamon and baking soda; stir into creamed mixture just until blended. Gently stir in raisins.
Press into ann 11 x 7 baking dish coated with cooking spray. Bake at 350° for 14-18 minutes or until set and edges are lightly brown. Cut into 12 bars.
