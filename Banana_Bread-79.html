Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup soft butter
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 ½ cups mashed bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup mini choc chips, optional

Directions

Beat butter, gradually add sugar. Blend in bananas, eggs and vanilla. Stir together dry ingredients and alternately with buttermilk. Fold in mini chips if using them. Grease and flour 4 small loaf pans. Bake at 350° for approximately 30-37 minutes.
