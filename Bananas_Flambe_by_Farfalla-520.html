Ingredients

  • makes 2 servings
  • 2 bananas cut @ angle
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 2 ounces unsalted butter, 1/2 stick
  • ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon Amaretto
  • ice cream-vanilla or cinnamon

Directions

In skillet, melt butter, add bananas. (medium to medium high heat) Stirring constantly until they caramelized. Add brown sugar and cinnamon. Add a GOOD quality Amaretto and light on fire. Serve immediately with ice cream.
