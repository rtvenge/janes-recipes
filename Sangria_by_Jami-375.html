Ingredients

  • 1.5 liter Yellow Tail cab or merot
  • 2 sliced pears, hard
  • 1 Breaburn apple-sliced
  • 1 orange , cut in 1/2 circles
  • 2 lemons, cut in full circles
  • 2 limes, cut in full circles
  • blueberries
  • 1 ½ cups triple sec
  • 2 cups sugar

Directions

Mix all together and refrigerate 12-24 hours.
