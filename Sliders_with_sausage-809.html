Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound sausage
  • 1 pound Velveeta Cheese
  • Sweet Hawaiian mini buns

Directions

Brown the meats together, drain. Add cheese. Put on buns and serve. Can also serve with diced onions that have been cooked in a bit of butter.
