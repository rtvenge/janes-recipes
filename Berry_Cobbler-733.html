Ingredients

  • 24 ounces frozen mixed berries with peaches
  • white cake mix
  • 1 can diet 7up, maybe a bit more or less

Directions

Place frozen berries in a 9x13 lightly greased pan. Top with dry cake mix. Pour 7up over the mix, do not leave any dry spots. Bake at 350° for 45-50 minutes.
