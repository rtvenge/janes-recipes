Buffalo Chicken Nachos

Ingredients

  • 3-4 boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup Frank's Wing Sauce, more or less
  • 4 green onions, thinly sliced
  • shredded lettuce
  • chopped tomato
  • ranch dressing
  • blue cheese or feta, crumbled
  • tortilla chips or thick potato chips
  • 3-4 cups cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions

Cook chicken and shred. Add onions to a fry pan with a bit of oil. Saute and add chicken and wing sauce. Cook until bubbly.
Spread chips on baking sheet. Top with chicken. Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese. Bake at 375° until cheese is melted. Remove from oven, add in this order
dressing
lettuce
tomato
feta/blue cheese
