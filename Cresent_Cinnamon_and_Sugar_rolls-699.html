Cresent Cinnamon and Sugar rolls

Ingredients

  • 1 tube crescent rolls
  • butter
  • cinnamon and sugar mixture
  • 1 ⅓ cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk

Directions

Spread butter on rolls, sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar. Roll up as cresent style rolls. Bake at 375° for 11-13 minutes. Spoon the milk/sugar frosting over each roll while hot.
