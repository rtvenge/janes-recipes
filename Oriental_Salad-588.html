Ingredients

  • one package package shredded coleslaw
  • one Ramen beef soup
  • ¼-½ cups sunflower seeds
  • 5 green onions, chopped
  • ½ cup oil
  • ¼ cup cider vinegar
  • ½ cup sugar
  • flavor pkg from soup mix

Directions

Combine all salad ingredients in large bowl. Combine dressing ingredients and pour over salad. Refrigerate for at least one hour before serving.
