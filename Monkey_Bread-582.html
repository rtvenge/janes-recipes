Ingredients

  • ⅔ cups sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon cinnamon
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • refrigerated buttermilk flaky biscuits

Directions

In small bowl, combine sugar and cinnamon. Mix well. Separate dough into 10 biscuits. Cut into fours. Dip in butter, then sugar mixture. Place in a 9x13 greased pan. Bake at 400° for 6-8 minutes. Serve warm.
