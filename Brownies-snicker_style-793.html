Ingredients

  • 4 ounces unsweetened chocolate, coarsely chopped
  • ¾ cup butter
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 3 eggs-beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • ¼ teaspoon kosher or regular table salt
  • 1 cup flour
  • fun size snickers candy bars, cut into fourths

Directions

Line a 8x8 pan with foil and spray with cooking spray.
Microwave chocolate and butter at 50% powder until butter is melted.
Whisk until chocolate is melted. Stir in sugar. Add eggs, extract, and salt. Gradually add flour and stir until combined.
Spread 1/2 the batter in pan. Add an even layer of Snickers and cover with remaining 1/2 the batter. Bake at 350° for 30-35 minutes. Do not overbake.
Remove to cool rack. cool before cutting.
