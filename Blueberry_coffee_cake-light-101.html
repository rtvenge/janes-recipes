Blueberry coffee cake-light

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons butter-soft
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 ⅓ cups low-fat buttermilk
  • 1 egg white
  • 2 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon turbinado sugar
  • cooking spray

Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350°.
2. Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Place granulated sugar and butter in large bowl. Beat at medium with mixer about 2 minutes. Add egg and egg white, beat well. Add flour mixture and buttermilk alternately. Mix after each addition. Spray a 9 inch round pan with spray. Add 1/2 the mixture. Sprinkle with 1 cup berries. Add remaining batter, then remaining berries. Sprinkle on top with turbinado sugar. Bake for 50 minutes or until it tests done. Cook on rack.
