Butterscotch Breakfast Rolls

Ingredients

  • 12 frozen dinner rolls
  • 1 package butterscotch pudding, not instant
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup butter, melted

Directions

In buttered bundt pan-add rolls. Cover with pudding, brown sugar and then butter. Cover with plastic wrap and let set overnight. In the morning-bake at 350° for 30 minutes. Invert on serving plate.
