Ingredients

  • 12 Hawaiian sweet or small dinner rolls
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 teaspoons poppy seeds
  • ⅓ pound shaved ham
  • 8 slices Swiss cheese

Directions

Melt butter in saute pan, add onion and saute. Add mustard, wsauce, poppyseeds. Line a 9x13 pan with foil. Slice open in a group the buns. Put the buns on foil. Add 1/2 the onion mixture, ham, cheese and top with 1/2 onion mixture. Top with bun tops. Cover with foil and refrigerate until ready. Bake at 350° for 20 minutes.
