Pumpkin Pie Martini

Ingredients

  • 1.5 ounces vanilla vodka
  • 3 ounces pumpkin pie puree
  • 1.5 ounces pumpkin pie syrup
  • 3.5 ounces half and half
  • 1 ounce milk
  • Graham cracker crumbs

Directions

Shake all ingredients together over ice. (Except crumbs) Rim your martini glass with crumbs and serve.
