Ingredients

  • 10 ounces fresh spinach
  • 16 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • ½ pound bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 3 celery ribs, sliced
  • 4 ounces cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 3 hard boiled eggs, chopped
  • 3 green onions, sliced
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • ¾ cup cider vinegar
  • ¾ cup oil
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Directions

Combine first seven ingredients in large salad bowl. Combine remaining ingredients in a jar, shake to blend. Drizzle over salad and serve immediately.
