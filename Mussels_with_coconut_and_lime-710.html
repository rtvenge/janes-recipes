Mussels with coconut and lime

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces can coconut milk
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 limes-zest and juice
  • 2 tablespoons grated fresh ginger
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 5 pounds mussels-cleaned and debearded
  • 1 cup minced fresh cilantro
  • 1 bunch green onions, thinly sliced

Directions

Put milk, onion, lime zest and juice, ginger, and soy sauce in a large pan with a tight fitting lid. Bring to boil over medium-high heat. Reduce to a simmer for about 5 minutes.
Toss mussels into the pot. Cover and steam for about 10 minutes. Discard any unopened mussels. Transfer to a serving bowl and cover with sauce and remaining ingredients.
