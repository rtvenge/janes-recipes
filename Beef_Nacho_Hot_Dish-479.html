Beef Nacho Hot Dish

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 ½ cups chunky salsa
  • 10 ounces frozen corn-thawed and drained
  • ¾ cup Miracle Whip
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 cups crushed tortilla chips
  • 2 cups Colby Cheese-shredded

Directions

Brown ground beef and drain. To the ground beef add, salsa, corn, Miracle Whip and chili powder. In a two quart casserole dish, layer the ground beef mixture, chips and cheese, TWICE, ending with the cheese on top.
Bake 20 minutes in a 350° oven, or until the dish is heated through.
