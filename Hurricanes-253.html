Ingredients

  • 1 cup white rum
  • 12 ounces can pineapple-orange juice
  • ½ cup grenadine
  • crushed ice
  • orange slices
  • Marachino Cherries

Directions

1. Combine rum, juice, and grenadine in a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake to combine.
2. Fill four hurricane glasses with crushed ice. Divide cocktail between glasses. Garnish each with orange slices and cherries.
