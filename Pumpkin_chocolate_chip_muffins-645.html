Pumpkin chocolate chip muffins

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • ¾ cup pumpkin
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • ¾ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon nutmet
  • ½ cups mini choc chips

Directions

Grease and flour muffin tins. Mix sugar, oil, and eggs. Add pumpkin and water. Mix together dry ingredients, add to wet mixture. Stir in chips.
Fill muffins 2/3 full. Bake at 400° for 20-25 minutes.
