Shrimp with tomatoes and feta

Ingredients

  • tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion-diced
  • 2 14.5 oz no salt diced tomatoes
  • ¼ cup flat leaf parsley
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill-minced
  • 1 ¼ pounds peeled shrimp
  • ¼ teaspoon plus more salt
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper-or more
  • ⅔ cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions

Preheat oven to 425°. Heat oil in ovenproof skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion and cook, stirring frequently, until soft. Add garlic; cook one minute. Add tomatoes and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and let sauce simmer for 5 minutes or until thickened. Remove skillet from burner. Stir in parsley, dill, shrimp, salt and pepper. Sprinkle feta over top. Place skillet in oven; bake until shrimp are cooked through and cheese has melted, approximately 12 minutes.
