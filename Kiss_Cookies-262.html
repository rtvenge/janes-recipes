Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter
  • ⅓ cup Peanut Butter
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 ¾ cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 bag kisses

Directions

1. Mix first six ingredients throughly.
2. Add dry ingredients
3. Roll into balls
4. roll in sugar
5. Bake 8 minutes at 375
6. Add Kisses
