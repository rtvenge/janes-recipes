Ingredients

  • 1.5 pound ground beef
  • 1 large egg-beaten
  • ½ cup Parmesan Cheese
  • ⅓ cup bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon grated garlic
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • ⅓ cup milk
  • ½ teaspoon oregano-dried
  • 2 tablespoons parsley-dried

Directions

Mix all ingredients together. Form into balls and bake at 350° for approximately 25 minutes.
