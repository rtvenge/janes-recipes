Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup brown sugar
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 ¾ cups lowfat Bisquick
  • ¾ cup skim milk
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 egg

Directions

Combine brown sugar and cinnamon for the topping. Mix together the remaining ingredients, pour into a 9" round cake pan sprayed with nonstick spray. Sprinkle with topping and bake at 375° for 18-23 minutes.
