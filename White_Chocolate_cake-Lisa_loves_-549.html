Ingredients

  • 1 white cake mix, no pudding
  • 1 instant white chocolate pudding
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ¾ cup oil
  • ¾ cup water
  • 2 eggs

Directions

Spray bundt pan baking spray. (has flour in it) Mix everything together, put in pan. Bake at 350° for 45-50 minutes. Cool in pan for 30 minutes. Flip out and cool on rack. Frost as desired or serve with raspberries and whip cream.
