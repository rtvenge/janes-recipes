Meatloaf with Salsa

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground sirloin
  • ½ cup salsa
  • ½ cup Italian dried bread crumbs
  • ½ cup cheddar cheese
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

In large bowl, combine all ingredients. Transfer to a loaf pan or form into a loaf and put on wire rack with a rimmed cookie sheet under the rack. Bake at 350° for approximately 45 minutes.
