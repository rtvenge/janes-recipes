Lemon salad dressing

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup fresh lemon juice, approximately 2 lemons
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper
  • ½ cup good quality olive oil

Directions

Whisk oil into the lemon juice. Add seasoning.
