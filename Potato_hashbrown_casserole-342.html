Potato hashbrown casserole

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds frozen hash browns
  • 1 10.75 oz cream of potato soup
  • 1 10.75 oz cream of celery soup
  • 16 ounces sour cream
  • ¼ cup milk
  • ½ cup onion-chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon pepper
  • ⅛ teaspoon dill weed
  • 1 ½ cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • paprika to taste

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°. Grease a 9 x 13 pan. Combine all ingredients except the cheese and paprika in large bowl. Spread in the greased pan. Top with the cheese and paprika. Bake 1-1 1/2 hours until bubbly and golden brown.
