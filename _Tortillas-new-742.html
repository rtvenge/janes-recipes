Ingredients

  • 3 cups Flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon table salt, heaping
  • 5 tablespoons shortening, lard, or unsalted butter
  • ¾ cup warm water

Directions

In processor fit with a dough blade, pulse flour powder and salt. Add fat process with quick pulses. With the processor running add the water slowly. Process for 30 seconds.
Divide into 12 balls and let rest for 10 minutes covered with a kitchen towel.
Roll or press out and grill each.
