Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup oil
  • 2 teaspoons soda
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups shredded zucchini
  • ½ bag mini choc chips

Directions

Mix all ingredients together and bake at 325° for 40-45 minutes if in large loaf pan. If using smaller pans, decrease the baking time.
