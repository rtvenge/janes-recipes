Ingredients

  • 12 ounces bock beer
  • ½ cup apple cider
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon celery seed
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

Mix all together and simmer 20 minutes.
