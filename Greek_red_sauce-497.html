Ingredients

  • 15 ounces tomato sauce
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon cumin
  • Tabasco sauce, to taste
  • 2 garlic cloves, grated
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Directions

Saute the garlic in olive oil. Add remaining ingredients. Simmer, serve over rice or chicken.
