Spinach/Strawberry salad

Ingredients

  • spinach
  • sliced strawberries
  • feta cheese
  • toasted slivered almonds
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 cup oil

Directions

Whisk together the ingredients from the garlic through the oil. Chill before serving.
