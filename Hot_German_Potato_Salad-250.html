Hot German Potato Salad

Ingredients

  • ¼ lb. bacon, diced
  • ¾ c. thinly sliced celery
  • ½ c. sliced green or chopped onion
  • 1 ½ tablespoons flour
  • ¾ c. water
  • ⅓ c. vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon prepared mustard
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • ¼ teaspoon celery seed
  • Dash of pepper
  • 4 c. sliced cooked potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley

Directions

Brown bacon. Remove bacon, save drippings. Add celery and onions. Cook until limp. Stir in next 8 ingredients, cook until thickened stirring constantly. Fold in potatoes, parsley and bacon. Cover and heat. Yields 6 servings.
