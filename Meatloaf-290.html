Ingredients

  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup shredded carrots
  • ¼ cup chopped celery
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ¾ cup crushed saltine crackers
  • ¼ cup ketchup
  • ⅛ cup fresh parsley
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 1 teaspoon ground mustard
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon pepper
  • 1 ¼ pounds ground beef
  • ¼ cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar

Directions

In butter, saute the onion, carrot and celery. Combine remaining ingredients except the 1/4 cup ketchup and brown sugar. Place on cookie sheet and top with the 1/4 cup ketchup and brown sugar that has been combined. Bake at 350° for approximately one hour. Let rest for 10 minutes.
