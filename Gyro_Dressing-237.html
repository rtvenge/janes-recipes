Ingredients

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ teaspoon dill weed
  • 1 clove garlic-minced
  • ¼ cucumber peeled,seeded, grated

Directions

Combine all ingredients and chill. If you use Greek yogurt the dressing is more flavorful.
