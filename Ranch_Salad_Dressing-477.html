Ingredients

  • 1 cup sour cream
  • ½ cup buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1 tablespoon dried chives
  • 1-1 ½ teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1-1 ½ teaspoons dried dill
  • 1 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • pepper to taste

Directions

Mix all together and refrigerate at least 2 hours before using.
