Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • ½ sm. onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ¾ teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper

Directions

Place all ingredients in the blender. Blend on medium speed for 15 seconds or until smooth. Cover and chill.
