Trish's Cherry Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 fudge cake mix
  • 21 ounces cherry pie filling
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 2 eggs-beaten
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 5 tablespoons butter
  • ⅓ cup milk
  • 1 cup semi sweet choc chips

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°. Grease 9x13 pan with shortening and flour. Combine the mix, filling, extract and eggs by hand. Pour into prepared pan. Bake 25-30 minutes. FROSTING: In small saucepan, combine all ingredients BUT the chips and boil. Stirring constantly for one minute. Remove from heat and add chips stirring until smooth. Pour over cake.
