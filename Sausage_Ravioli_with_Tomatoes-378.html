Sausage Ravioli with Tomatoes

Ingredients

  • 9 ounce package refrigerated Italian Sausage-filled ravioli
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, cut into 1/4 inch wedges
  • ½ cup green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 14 oz can diced pealed tomatoes, undrained
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil leaves
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • dash cayenne pepper

Directions

1. Cook ravioli to desired doneness as directed on package. drain; keep warm
2. Meanwhile, heat oil in large skillet over medium high heat. Add onion, bell pepper and garlic; cook 3 to 5 minutes or until vegetables are crisp-tender,stirring occasionally. Add tomatoes, basil, salt and ground red pepper. bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer 3 to 5 minutes or until sauce is slightly thickened. Stir in cooked ravioli; cook an additional 2 to 4 minutes or until thoroughly heated, stirring frequently.
