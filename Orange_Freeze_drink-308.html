Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 6 oz can frozen orange juice
  • ½ cup 1/2 and 1/2
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • ¼ cup rum or white tequila
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 14 ice cubes

Directions

Blend all in a blender and serve immediately. Garnish with orange wedge and red cherry.
