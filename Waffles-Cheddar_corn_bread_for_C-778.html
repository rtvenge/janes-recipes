Waffles-Cheddar corn bread for Chili

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1.5 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup club soda
  • ⅓ cup canola oil
  • cooking spray

Directions

Sift the first five ingredients together. Stir in cheese. Combine eggs and next three ingredients. Gently stir into dry ingredients. Spray waffle iron with cooking spray. Cook 3-5 minutes. Transfer waffles to a baking sheet, uncovered in a 200° oven to keep warm. To serve, put waffle on a plate. Top with chili, cheddar cheese, green onion, and sour cream.
