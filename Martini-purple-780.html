Ingredients

  • 3 ounces vodka
  • 1.5 ounces cranberry juice
  • ½ ounce blue Curacoao
  • ½ ounce sweet and sour
  • ½ ounce 7 up

Directions

Put in shaker with ice. If not purple enough, add more blue Curacao.
