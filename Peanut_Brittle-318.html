Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 12 ounces milk choc chips
  • 3.5 ounces cashew pieces

Directions

Bring to fast boil, over medium high heat, boil to 300°. Butter a cookie sheet, pour over cashews, frost with chips
