Ingredients

  • 3 cups self-rising flour
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 1 can beer

Directions

Mxi together all ingredients, then place in two greased loaf pans. Pour melted butter on top. Bake at 350° for 50-60 minutes.
