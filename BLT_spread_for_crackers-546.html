Ingredients

  • 6 slices cooked and crumbled bacon
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 1 cup plum tomatoes, seeded and chopped

Directions

Mix all together. Refrigerate for an hour and serve with crackers.
