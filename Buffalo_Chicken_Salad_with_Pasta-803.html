Ingredients

  • 1 Marie's blue cheese dressing, jar
  • ¾ Frank's Wing Sauce, of the bottle
  • 5 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 1 Rotisserie chicken, deboned, skinned and chopped
  • 1 16 oz rotini pasta, box

Directions

Mix the wing sauce and blue cheese dressing together. Cook the pasta. Mix all together and refrigerate for at least 2 hours to blend flavors.
