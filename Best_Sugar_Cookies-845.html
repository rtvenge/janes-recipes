Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1.5 teaspoons almond extract
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2.5 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2-4 tablespoon 2% milk

Directions

Cream together butter and sugar. Add extracts and egg until blended. Add flour and salt. Mix until blended. Chill for 30 minutes or more. Roll out and bake at 375° for 7-9 minutes. Frost.

Frosting-mix together butter, sugar and vanilla. Add milk to best consistancy.
