Ingredients

  • 1 ounce vodka
  • 1 ounce triple sec
  • 1 ounce peach schnapps
  • cranberry juice , splash
  • club soda-chilled
  • lime wedges

Directions

Mix the liquor ingredients in a shaker. Pour over ice. Add a splash of cranberry juice. Top with club soda, garnish with a lime wedge.
