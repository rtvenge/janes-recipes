Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter-soft
  • ½ cup shortening
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 ¼ cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 12 ounces chocolate chips

Directions

Preheat oven 375°. Beat butter, shortening and sugars until fluffy. Add eggs and vanilla, beat until smooth. Stir in dry ingredients. Add 12 oz of chocolate chips. Bake 9-11 minutes.
