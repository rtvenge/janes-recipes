Banana Split Bread

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup shortening
  • 1 ¼ cups sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 3.5 cups flour
  • 2.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1.5 cups mashed bananas
  • 16 ounces crushed pineapple , canned-drained
  • 12 ounces chocolate chips
  • 10 ounces red marachini cherries, drained and chopped

Directions

Cream shortening and sugar. Add eggs, one at a time, beating after each. Combine dry ingredients and add to mixture alternately with bananas and pineapple. Fold in chips and cherries.
Pour into two greased loaf pans. Bake at 350° for 60-65 minutes. Cool 10 minutes before removing from pans.
