Ingredients

  • ½ refrigerated sugar cookie dough, tube
  • ¼ cup flour
  • 24 miniature Snicker Bars
  • red and green colored sugar

Directions

In a bowl, beat cookie dough and flour until combined. Shape 1.5 teaspoonfuls of dough around each candy bar. Roll in colored sugar. Either green or red.
Place 2 inches apart on parchment paper-lined baking sheets. Bake at 350° for 10-12 minutes.
