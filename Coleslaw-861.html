Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1.5 tablespoons cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • ¾ teaspoon celery seed
  • 1 teaspoon kosher or regular table salt
  • ½ teaspoon fresh ground pepper

Directions

Whisk together and pour over cabbage/carrot mixture.
