Strawberry Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups strawberries, sliced/1.5 cups mashed
  • 1 ½ cups oil
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup sifted confectioners' sugar
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla

Directions

Beat eggs with mixer on high for 4-5 minutes. Add sugar, beat one minute. Add mashed berries and oil, beating on low to combine. Mix together dry ingredients and add to berry mixture, stir just until blended.
Put in two greased and floured loaf pans. Bake at 350° for approximately 40 minutes. Cool in pan 10 minutes. Remove from pans and cool completely.
Mix the frosting ingredients, tint with pink food coloring if desired and drizzle over both loaves.
