Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • 2 ounces cream cheese
  • 1 green onion, bunch
  • fresh dill, chopped
  • flat leaf parsley, chopped
  • 1-2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zest
  • ½ unpeeled English cucumber, grated

Directions

Mix all in processor except the cucumber. Add cuc and pulse.
