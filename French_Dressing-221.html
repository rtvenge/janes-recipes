Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 cup chili sauce
  • 1 cup catsup
  • 1 cup oil

Directions

Place all ingredients in blender and store in refrigerator.
