Sangria with rum

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 lime , sliced
  • 1 orange , sliced
  • 1 ½ cups rum
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 750 ML dry red wine, bottle
  • 1 cup orange juice

Directions

Chill all liquids very well. Stir together fruit, rum and sugar. Chill for 2 hours.
Lightly crush fruit with spoon, add remaining ingredients and serve.
