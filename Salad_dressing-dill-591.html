Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • ¼ cup oil
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons sour cream
  • 2 teaspoons dill weed
  • salt to taste

Directions

Whisk together and serve over your favorite salad.
