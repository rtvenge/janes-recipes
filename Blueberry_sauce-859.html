Ingredients

  • 2 cups frozen or fresh blueberries
  • juice of one lemon
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions

Heat berries in pan over medium heat. Add juice, sugar, and cinnamon. Bring to boil. Reduce heat, simmer, stirring 10 minutes. Serve warm.
