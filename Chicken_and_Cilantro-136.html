Chicken and Cilantro

Ingredients

  • ½ cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic-minced
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • ½ teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 teaspoons olive oil
  • 4 chicken breast
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pound green beans
  • 4 Roma tomatoes, cut 1/2 length wise

Directions

1. In a bowl mix the beans and tomatoes with 2 teaspoons oil, with 1/4 teaspoon of salt and pepper.
2. Grill the beans and tomatoes for 3-5 minutes, turning frequently.
3. Rub the chicken with 1/2teaspoon of salt, pepper and 1 teaspoon oil and grill.
4. In a bowl for the dressing mix the cilantro, garlic, lime, sugar and 1 tablespoon of oil.
5. Serve the dressing over the chicken and bean/tomato mixture.
