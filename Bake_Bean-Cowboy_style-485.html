Ingredients

  • 8 ounces bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 pound ground beef, drained
  • ½ cup onion, chopped
  • 24 ounces pinto beans-canned
  • 31 ounces butter beans-canned
  • 30 ounces baked beans with pork-canned
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1 cup brown sugar

Directions

Combine all ingredients in crock pot except the bacon. Cook on high for 3-4 hours. Add bacon before serving.
