Ingredients

  • ¾ pound ziti pasta
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 ounces cream cheese
  • 1.5 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Directions

Cook pasta. Brown beef and drain. Add seasoning and the water. Add cream cheese and blend. Add pasta and one cup of the shredded cheese. Put in a lightly sprayed casserole dish, top with the 1/2 cup of cheese. Bake at 350° for approximately 30 minutes.
