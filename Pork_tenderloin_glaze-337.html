Pork tenderloin glaze

Ingredients

  • ½ cup seedless red raspberry jam
  • 6 tablespoons raspberry vinegar
  • ¼ cup Dijon mustard

Directions

In saucepan, stir together all ingredients. Bring to a boil over high heat; cook and stir 3 minutes. Serve over pork tenderloin or chicken.
