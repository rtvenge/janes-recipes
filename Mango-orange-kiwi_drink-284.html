Ingredients

  • 4 large kiwi-peeled/chopped
  • 2 large mangos-peeled/chopped
  • 1 pint orange juice
  • 3 cups cold water
  • vodka

Directions

Using a blender, puree the fruits and orange juice. Pour into pitcher, add water and stir. Add vodka as wanted or use as a virgin drink.
