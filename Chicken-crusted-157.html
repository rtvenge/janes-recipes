Ingredients

  • 2 eggs-beaten
  • 1 cup Ritz cracker crumbs
  • ½ teaspoon garlic salt
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper
  • 4 chicken breast
  • ½ cup butter-cut into pieces

Directions

Preheat oven to 375°. In a shallow bowl, add eggs. In a second shallow bowl, add the crumbs with the spices. Dip the chicken in the eggs, then the crumbs. Put in 9 x 13 pan. Put the butter pieces around the chicken. Bake for 40 minutes.
