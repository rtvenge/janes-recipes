Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds lean ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1 cup 2% milk
  • 1 Tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon sage
  • 1 egg-beaten
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon dry mustard
  • 2 cloves garlic-grated
  • 1 small onion-chopped
  • ½ cup dry bread crumbs
  • ⅓ cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons brown sugar

Directions

Combine all ingredients together except the last three. Form into a loaf form and place on a broiler style pan. Combine last three ingredients and cover the loaf. Bake for 1 1/4 hours at 350°. Let set 10 minutes before slicing.
