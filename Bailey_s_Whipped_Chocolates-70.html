Ingredients

  • 36 premade chocolate cordials, Available at Byerlys or Kowalskis
  • 1 cup heavy whip cream
  • ⅓ cup Baileys
  • 6 ounces good quality chocolate chips

Directions

In saucepan, heat cream to almost boiling;, add chocolate chips and whisk until smooth. Add Baileys and put in mixing bowl. Refrigerate 40-45 minutes. With mixer, beat chocolate until fluffy. Put in pastry bag and fill each cup. Refrigerate.
