baked beans with cranberry

Ingredients

  • 28 oz canned baked beans
  • 1 cup Byerlys North Woods Maple Rosemary grilling sauce
  • 1 cup diced red onion
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup dried cranberries
  • 6 slices applewood smoked bacon, cut into 1/2 inch pieces

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°. In casserole, combine beans, sauce, onion, brown sugar and cranberries. Top with bacon; cover and bake one hour.
Remove cover; increase oven temperature to 425°. Bake until bacon is crisp. Approximately 20 minutes. Let me 15 minutes before serving.
