Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups cranberry juice
  • ½ cup Grand Mariner
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lim juice
  • prosecco
  • lime strips

Directions

In pitcher, combine juice, Grand Mariner and lime juice. Pour in glasses over ice, top with prosecco. Garnish with lime strip. Can be doubled.
