Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • pepper to taste
  • ½ cup butter

Directions

✱ Grate cheese, cut butter into quarters. Combine cheese, cream and pepper, cook on low and slowly add butter, stir constantly. Do NOT boil.
