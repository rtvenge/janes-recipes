Ingredients

  • 28 ounces canned tomatoes
  • 2 10 oz ea Rotel tomatoes with gr. chiles
  • ¼ cup onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno, chopped
  • ½ cup cilantro
  • 1/2 lime , juiced
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon cumin

Directions

Combine in blender and refrigerate.
