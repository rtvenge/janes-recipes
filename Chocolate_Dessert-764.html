Ingredients

  • 1 can full fat coconut milk
  • ¼ cup cocoa powder, plus 1 tablespoon
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla
  • powdered sugar, to taste

Directions

Put milk in a bowl and refrigerate uncovered overnight. Do not shake can before opening. Thai Kitchen Organic is the best brand. Can drain a bit of the water in the bowl. Whip in cocoa, vanilla, and sugar with a fork. Store in refrigerator uncovered. Will thicken.
