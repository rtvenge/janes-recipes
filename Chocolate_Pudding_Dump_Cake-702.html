Chocolate Pudding Dump Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 chocolate cake mix, box
  • 1 chocolate pudding-instant, small box
  • 1.5 cups skim milk
  • 1 ½ cups chocolate chips

Directions

Mix pudding with the milk. After mixing the pudding, mix with the dry cake mix. Batter will be very thick. Spread into a greased 9x13 pan. Sprinkle with chips. Bake at 350° for 25-30 minutes or until the cake pulls away from the pan.
