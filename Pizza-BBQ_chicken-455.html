Ingredients

  • pizza crust
  • ½ cup BBQ sauce
  • ½ cup grilled onions
  • ½ cup grilled diced chicken
  • ¼ cup red bell pepper, chopped
  • ¼ cup green bell pepper, chopped
  • ¼ cup red onion, chopped
  • 1 cup Monterey Jack cheese, shredded

Directions

Preheat oven to 450°.

Place pizza crust on pizza stone. Spread with BBQ sauce. Scatter chicknen, peppers and onion. Cover with cheese.

Bake for 10-12 minutes or until cheese is melted.
