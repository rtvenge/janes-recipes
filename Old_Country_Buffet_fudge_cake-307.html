Old Country Buffet fudge cake

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups sugar-divided
  • 1 cup flour
  • 7 tablespoons cocoa-divided
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup milk
  • ⅓ cup butter-melted
  • 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups hot water
  • whipped topping

Directions

1. Heat oven 350°. Stir together 3/4 cup sugar, flour, 3 tablespoons cocoa, baking powder and salt. Stir in milk, butter and vanilla; beat until smooth.
2. Pour batter into ungreased 9 inch square pan. Stir together remaining 1/2 cup sugar, brown sugar and 4 tablespoon cocoa; sprinkle mixture evenly over batter. Pour hot water over top BUT DO NOT STIR. Bake 35-40 minutes or until center is set. Let stand 15 minutes. Serve individually and top with whip cream mixture.
