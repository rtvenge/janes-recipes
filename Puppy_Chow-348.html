Ingredients

  • 12 ounces Crispix cereal
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 12 ounces chocolate chips
  • 2-3 cups powdered sugar

Directions

Melt together in microwave, butter, peanut butter and chocolate chips. Pour over cereal. Put cereal in a paper bag, add powdered sugar and shake gently to coat.
