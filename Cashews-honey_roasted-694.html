Cashews-honey roasted

Ingredients

  • 1 pound cashews
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1.5 tablespoons REAL maple syrup
  • 1.5 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Directions

Preheat oven to 350° and line a baking sheet with foil. Microwave honey,butter, syrup, and butter 25-30 seconds or until butter is melted. Add salt, vanilla, and cinnamon and stir. Pour over cashews and mix to coat. Bake, single layer for 6 minutes. Remove from oven, toss to mix and bake 6 minutes longer. Remove and put in a bowl, toss with the sugar and a bit of salt. Let cool, stirring occasionally. When cool, store in an air-tight container.
