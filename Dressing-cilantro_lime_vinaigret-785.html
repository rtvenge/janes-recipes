Ingredients

  • Juice of lime, approx 2 T
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ¼ cup cilantro
  • ½ cup EVOO

Directions

In processor, add lime juice, vinegar, and salt. Pulse a few times. Add garlic and cilantro and pulse until finely chopped. Drizzle in EVOO. Shake before serving.
