Chocolate Snowballs

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup butter, softened
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup caramel chips
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • powdered sugar

Directions

Cream butter and sugar. Add egg and vanilla until combined. Add flour and salt. Beat until blended. Stir in chips. Bake at 350° for 15-20 minutes. When slightly cool, roll in powdered sugar.
