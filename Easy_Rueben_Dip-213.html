Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Swiss Cheese, cut up into cubes
  • 1 cup Thousand Island Dressing
  • 1 16-oz. can Sauerkraut, drained
  • 1 large package (or two small packages Carl Budding corned beef, cut up
  • Party rye bread

Directions

Combine all ingredients (except the bread) in a crock pot. Heat for 1 hour. Stir often. Serve on party rye bread.
