Ingredients

  • 1 Good Seasonings dry Italian dressing package
  • ½ teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ tablespoon mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1.5 tablespoons water

Directions

Make the Good Seasoning dressing according to their instructions. Add the remaining ingredients. Refrigerate up to four weeks.
