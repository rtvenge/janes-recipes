Broccoli salad with raisins

Ingredients

  • 10 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 broccoli, large cluster
  • ¼ cup red onion, finely chopped
  • ½ cup raisins
  • 3 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 cup sunflower seeds

Directions

Cut broccoli into pieces. Add the onion and raisins. For the dressing combine, vinegar, sugar and mayo. Mix together and refrigerate. Before serving, add the bacon and seeds.
