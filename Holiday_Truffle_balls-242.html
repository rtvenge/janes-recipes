Holiday Truffle balls

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup whipping cream
  • 8 ounces semisweet baking chocolate, chopped
  • ⅓ cup soft butter
  • unsweetened cocoa

Directions

In saucepan, combine whip cream, chocolate and cook over low heat, stir occasionally for 7-9 minutes or until chocolate melts. Remove from heat, add butter. Cover and refrigerate two hours. Dust hands with cocoa and roll into balls. Roll in cocoa.
