Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ½ teaspoon dried parsley
  • ½ teaspoon dill
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon pepper

Directions

Whisk all ingredients together. Refrigerate for at least an hour for flavors to blend.
