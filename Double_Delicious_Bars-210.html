Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 14 ounces sweetened condensed milk
  • 12 ounces chocolate chips
  • 12 ounces peanut butter chips

Directions

Preheat oven to 350° or if using a glass pan, preheat oven to 325°. In a 13x9 pan, melt the butter in the pan. (in the oven) Sprinkle crumbs evenly over the butter; pour milk over the crumbs. Top with the chips; press down firmly. Bake 25-30 minutes or until light browned. Cool. Cut into bars.
