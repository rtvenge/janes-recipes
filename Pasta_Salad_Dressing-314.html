Ingredients

  • ¾ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • ¾ cup wine vinegar
  • ½ cup 1/2 and 1/2
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 2 cloves garlic-minced

Directions

Combine all ingredients in blender for one minute. Serve over any pasta of your choice, with an accent of green onion, green pepper, cubed provolone cheese etc.
