Cuban pork chop rub

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons grated lime peel
  • 1 tablespoon fresh black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon cumin seed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 4 pork chops

Directions

Combine all the ingredients in a small bowl. Rub both sides of pork chops with rub. Refrigerate 2-4 hours. Bring to room temperature before grilling.
