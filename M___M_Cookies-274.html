Ingredients

  • ¾ cup butter crisco
  • 1 ⅓ cups brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 ¼ cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • M & Ms

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°. Beat crisco and sugar, add eggs and vanilla. Add dry ingredients. Put on baking sheet and over with 6 M & Ms. Bake for approximately 12 minutes.
