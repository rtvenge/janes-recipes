Ingredients

  • 1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can corn, drained
  • 1 bag of cole slaw cabbage
  • ⅓ cup green onion, chopped
  • ¼ cup cilantro, fresh and chopped
  • 1 cup tomato , chopped
  • 1 avacado , chopped
  • ¾ cup Jalapeno ranch dressing
  • Dressing
  • 3 tomatillos, husked and quartered
  • 1/2 bunch cilantro
  • 2 pickled jalapenos, whole
  • 16 ounces sour cream
  • 2 1 oz dry Ranch dressing pkgs

Directions

Mix all the salad ingredients. For the dressing, process all under incorporated.
