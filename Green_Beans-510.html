Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh cooked green beans
  • ¼ cup rice vinegar
  • ¼ cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dill
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Directions

Combine all ingredients (not beans), pour over beans and serve.
