Ingredients

  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • ⅔ cup sugar
  • ⅓ cup cider vinegar
  • ⅓ cup canola oil
  • 4 teaspoons prepared mustard
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • pepper to taste
  • poppy seeds to taste, optional

Directions

Put everything in a blender, except the poppy seeds. Blend until mixed throughly. Add poppy seeds to blend.
