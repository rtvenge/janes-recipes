Cranberry chicken

Ingredients

  • 6 boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 16 ounces whole cranberry sauce
  • 8 ounces Western Dressing
  • 1 dry onion soup mix, package

Directions

Make sauce of the three ingredients. Pour cover chicken breasts in a baking dish. Bake at 300° for 1 1/2 hours covered, uncover and continue to bake for 1/2 hour longer.
