Pork Tenderlion with apple stuffing

Ingredients

  • 1 pork tenderloin
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup onion, chopped
  • 1 tart apple , peeled and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • ½ cup apple juice or cider, or more
  • Rib recipe
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon paparika
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced

Directions

Rub tenderloin with rub mixture. Brown in olive oil on all sides. Remove from pan, add apple and onion. Saute until tender. Add the mustard and sugar. Cut the tenderloin like a book. Fill with apple mixture. It will not close but tie with string to hold together. Top will be open. Put on oven safe dish. Pour the juice around the meat. Bake 400 ° until done to your liking.
