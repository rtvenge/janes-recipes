Cranberry Pomegrante Relish

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pomegranate juice
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon orange zest
  • ½ teaspoon cardamon-ground
  • 12 ounces fresh cranberries

Directions

Boil juice, sugar, zest and spice until sugar is dissolved.
Add berries, simmer until they burst. Chill.
