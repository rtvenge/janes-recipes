Ingredients

  • Turkey
  • dollar buns/ham buns
  • provolone cheese
  • 1 cup butter
  • 4 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons mustard
  • 2 teaspoons poppy seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Directions

Make sandwiches and place in 9x13 pan. Mix together butter, sugar, mustard, poppy seeds and W sauce. Slowly pour the mixture to evenly coat sandwiches. Cover and refrigerate over night or at least a few hours. Bake covered at 325° for 20 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 5 minutes.
