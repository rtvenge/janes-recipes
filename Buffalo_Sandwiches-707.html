Ingredients

  • 3 pounds bonesless skinless chicken breasts
  • 12 ounces Frank's Wing Sauce
  • 1 ounce Hidden Valley Ranch envelope
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Directions

In crock pot, put in breasts, sauce, dry dressing. Cover, cook on low 7-9 hours. Shred chicken, return to crock pot and add butter. Heat and serve on buns.
