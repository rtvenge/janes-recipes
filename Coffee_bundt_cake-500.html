Coffee bundt cake

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup butter
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ cups sour cream
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup mini choc chips
  • 1 ½ teaspoons cinnamon

Directions

Grease pan. Cream together butter and sugar. Add egg and vanilla. Beat until smooth. Alternate the sour cream and dry ingredients. Pour one third of the batter into the pan. Mix together the brown sugar, chips and cinnamon. Add half of the brown sugar mixture. Add one third of the batter. Add the remaining brown sugar mixture. Add remaining batter. Bake at 350° for approximately one hour.
