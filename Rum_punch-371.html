Rum punch

Ingredients

  • 2 cups unsweetened apple juice
  • 1 ½ cups unsweetened pineapple juice
  • 1 12 0z can frozen cranberry juice concentrate, thawed
  • 1 6 0z can frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
  • 1 cup golden or light rum
  • 1 liter club soda-chilled
  • pineapple wedges for garnish

Directions

1. In large pitcher or bowl, combine apple juice, pineapple juice, cranberry and orange juice concentrates and rum. Chill.
2. Just before serving, add club soda, serve in hurricane style glasses over ice and garnish with pineapple wedge.
