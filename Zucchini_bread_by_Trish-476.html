Ingredients

  • 2 cups zucchini, grated and drained one hour/squeezed dry
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 3 teaspoons vanilla
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup mini choc chips

Directions

Mix all ingredients together except the chips. Fold in chips. Pour into 2 loaf pans that have been greased. Bake at 325° for approximately 45 minutes.
