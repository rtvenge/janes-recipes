Ingredients

  • 1 avacado
  • 1 jalapeno
  • ¼ cup cilantro
  • green onion
  • ¾ cup buttermilk
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise, optional
  • ½ teaspoon cumin seeds, toasted and ground
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

Combine all ingredients in the blender.
