Ingredients

  • ½ cup chili powder
  • ½ cup sweet paprika
  • ½ cup coriander
  • ½ cup cumin
  • ½ cup fennel seeds-crushed
  • ½ cup celery seeds-crushed
  • 1 tablespoon ground cardamom
  • 1 tablespoon garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper

Directions

Combine all ingredients in a small bowl and mix thoroughly.
